# coldemailrepo
Cold Email Generator AI is a tool that uses artificial intelligence (like ChatGPT) to automatically write personalized sales outreach emails. You feed it details about your prospect and goal, and it creates a ready-to-send draft that sounds human, saves time, and aims to get more replies.

 A live job link is required to generate the email for the required job description,analysed by AI. The email can be downloaded and customised as per the user.The skills and roles can also be modified according to needs.
 Role,description,and experience are summarised by AI.Relevant portfolio links are provided.
